

11. HOW THESE WORDS WORK TOGETHER ?
___________________________________________

        1. Producer sends event to topic.
        2. Topic is divided into partitions.
        3. partitions are stored on brokers.
        4. offsets track message position inside partition.
        5. consumer group reads partitions.
        6. lag shows how much consumer is behind.
        7. retention keeps old messages for replay.
        8. replication keeps data safer across brokers.
        9. rebalance happens when consumer group members change.

        Simple flow example:
        1. producer sends dealer-updated event
        2. broker stores it in dealer-events topic partition 1
        3. message gets offset 55
        4. audit-service consumer group reads offset 55
        5. analytics-service another group also reads same event
        6. if one consumer is slow, lag increases
        7. if one broker fails, replica helps
