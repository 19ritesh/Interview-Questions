

10. REBALANCE ?
___________________________________________

        1. Rebalance means Kafka reassigns partitions among consumers in one group.
        2. this happens when new consumer joins, one consumer leaves, or topic partitions change.
        3. during rebalance, some small pause can happen.
        4. after rebalance, partition ownership changes.
        5. this is normal behavior, but too much rebalance is not good.

        Example:
        1. group has 2 consumers first
        2. later 3rd consumer joins
        3. Kafka again distributes partitions across 3 consumers

        Code snippet:

                           kafkaConsumer.subscribe(List.of("dealer-events"));   // when group membership changes, rebalance can happen
