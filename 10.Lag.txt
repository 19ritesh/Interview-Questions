


8. LAG ?
___________________________________________

        1. Lag means consumer is behind producer.
        2. simple words, pending unread messages count.
        3. if latest offset is 500 and consumer committed 450, lag is around 50.
        4. high lag means consumer is slow or producer traffic is high.
        5. in production, lag monitoring is very important.

        Example:
        1. producer keeps writing quickly
        2. consumer processes slowly
        3. backlog grows
        4. this backlog is lag
